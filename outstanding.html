Outstanding Impact Program Overview

The Outstanding Impact program is designed to help you build the skills and experiences that will shape your future. As a UC scholar, this program supports your growth academically, personally, and professionally.

By participating in our events, you'll develop leadership skills, connect with industry experts, and gain hands-on experience—all while contributing to something bigger than yourself. This is your opportunity to refine the tools needed to make a lasting impact and succeed in your future career.
