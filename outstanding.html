Outstanding Impact Programme

The Outstanding Impact Programme is designed to nurture high-achieving students and provide them with the opportunities needed to excel in their academic and professional careers. As a recipient of the Outstanding Impact Scholarship, you'll be part of a prestigious network that connects you with industry professionals, mentors, and peers who share your passion for innovation and excellence. Through tailored workshops, networking events, and development activities, the programme focuses on empowering you to reach your full potential, build essential career skills, and create meaningful connections that will help shape your future success.

Kaitaki Ākonga (Student Leaders)

Our Kaitaki Ākonga are upper-year students who play a key role in leading and shaping the Outstanding Impact Programme. You’ll see them at all our events—facilitating leadership nights, supporting behind the scenes, and helping drive our kaupapa forward. These outstanding individuals are here to support, guide, and mentor you—so if you ever need a hand or some advice, don’t hesitate to reach out.
